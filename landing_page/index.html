ScratchPad

Ask the world for
feedback on your design.

Your Design Will Be
Reviewed By Turkers on MTurk.

ScratchPad offers a platform to gather useful heuristics data on your designs and then emails you the gathered data and insights.

Real Time Interaction

Is your design on the web? Users will be able to interact with it in real time through the ScratchPad platform and then be able to provide quantitative and qualitative feedback.

Quick Access to Feedback Data

We will email you the heuristics evaluation data we gather from participants who test your design, in readable format, within 24 hours after the task finishes on MTurk. You will also be sent a link where you can see substantial analysis of the data on our front-end.

Data Integration and Insights

Along with the data, ScratchPad will also provide useful insights and ways to integrate the insights into your design in order to improve it.

ScratchPad is for Everyone.

Simply upload any design mockup on the web and submit the link to ScratchPad to gather feedback. Insert some questions about your design to ensure Turkers substantially interact with your design.
